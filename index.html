Cost: 500 energy 300 Materials 20 energy/second

The Leidenfrost effect describes the chemical reaction that occurs when a liquid comes into contact with an extremely hot surface (more than 160°). The liquid will levitate 0.1mm above the surface. This levitating liquid, placed in a concave bowl, will move very quickly, this cinetic force will be able to create electricity. On Mars, dry ice is present in very large quantities, it is almost a raw material, to exploit the Leidenfrost effect.
